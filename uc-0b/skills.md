\# Skills: Policy Understanding and Compression



\## Core Skills



\### 1. Clause Extraction

\- Identify all clauses in the document

\- Map each clause to its obligation



\### 2. Obligation Preservation

\- Preserve strong words like:

&#x20; - must

&#x20; - requires

&#x20; - not permitted

\- Never weaken policy language



\### 3. Condition Handling

\- Capture conditions like:

&#x20; - approvals

&#x20; - deadlines

&#x20; - exceptions



\### 4. Risk Awareness

\- Avoid:

&#x20; - missing approvals

&#x20; - removing constraints

&#x20; - changing meaning



\### 5. Structured Summarization

\- Convert text into bullet points

\- Keep it clean and readable
