# Skills: UC-0B Policy Summarization

## Skill: `retrieve_policy`

### Role
You act as a data ingestion module ensuring that the entire policy text file is deeply read and safely loaded without unapproved truncation.

### Instructions
1. Accept the `file_path` of the policy text file (`../data/policy-documents/policy_hr_leave.txt`).
2. Read the entire document into memory.
3. Parse the text to separate clauses based on their specific numbering schema (e.g., 2.3, 2.4, etc.).
4. Return a structured list of objects where each contains the `clause_id` and raw `text`.

### Context
- The input is a raw `.txt` file containing numbered lists.
- You must perfectly preserve the textual logic and binding statements without altering the grammatical text.

### Expectations
- **Input:** `retrieve_policy("../data/policy-documents/policy_hr_leave.txt")`
- **Output:** `[{"clause_id": "2.3", "text": "14-day advance notice required..."}]`


## Skill: `summarize_policy`

### Role
You are the summarization engine strictly applying the non-lossy transformation on legal and policy texts without 'scope bleed' or 'obligation softening'.

### Instructions
1. Accept the structured list of clauses generated by `retrieve_policy`.
2. Iterate through the exact target clauses (2.3, 2.4, 2.5, 2.6, 2.7, 3.2, 3.4, 5.2, 5.3, 7.2).
3. Apply rigorous summarization ensuring NO conditions are lost.
4. If multi-approvers are required (like Clause 5.2 requiring the Department Head AND the HR Director), ensure all are explicitly captured.
5. Format the result cleanly as a compliant text summary with clause references.

### Context
- Must strictly strictly enforce the 4 primary rules: 
  1. Every numbered clause must be present.
  2. Preserve ALL conditions for multi-condition obligations.
  3. Never add information not present in the source document.
  4. If a clause cannot be summarised without meaning loss, quote it verbatim and flag it.

### Expectations
- **Input:** `summarize_policy([{"clause_id": "5.2", "text": "LWP requires Department Head AND HR Director approval"}])`
- **Output:** `"Clause 5.2: LWP requires dual approval from the Department Head and the HR Director."`
