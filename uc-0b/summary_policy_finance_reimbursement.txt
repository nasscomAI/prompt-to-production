HR LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY (STRICT BINDING)
==================================================
Clause 1.1: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] This policy governs reimbursement of expenses incurred by CMC employees in the course of official duties.
Clause 1.2: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Personal expenses are not reimbursable under any circumstances.
Clause 1.3: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] All claims must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the expense being incurred. Claims submitted after 30 days will not be processed.
Clause 2.1: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Local travel (within city limits) is reimbursable at actual cost for public transport or at Rs 4 per km for personal vehicle use. Receipts are required for all claims above Rs 200.
Clause 2.2: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Outstation travel must be pre-approved using Form FIN-T1 before travel commences. Travel without prior approval is not reimbursable.
Clause 2.3: 14-day advance notice MUST be provided for leave applications.
Clause 2.4: Written approval from direct manager MUST be received before leave commences. Verbal approval is invalid.
Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence WILL be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP), regardless of any later approval.
Clause 2.6: Employees MAY carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days. Anything above 5 days ARE FORFEITED on 31 December.
Clause 3.1: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Employees approved for permanent work-from-home arrangements are entitled to a one-time home office equipment allowance of Rs 8,000.
Clause 3.2: Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days REQUIRES a medical certificate submitted within 48 hours of return.
Clause 3.3: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] The allowance does not cover: personal computers, laptops, smartphones, printers, or air conditioning equipment.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave adjacent to a public holiday or annual leave REQUIRES a medical certificate unconditionally regardless of duration.
Clause 3.5: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Employees on temporary or partial work-from-home arrangements are not eligible for this allowance.
Clause 4.1: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Training expenses are reimbursable only if the training was pre-approved by the Department Head using Form FIN-TR1.
Clause 4.2: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Course fees are reimbursable up to Rs 15,000 per financial year per employee.
Clause 4.3: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Exam fees for professional certifications are reimbursable once per attempt per certification, up to Rs 5,000.
Clause 4.4: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] If an employee leaves CMC within 12 months of a reimbursed training expense, they must repay 100% of the reimbursed amount. If they leave between 12 and 24 months, they must repay 50%.
Clause 5.1: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Employees in Grade C and above are entitled to a monthly mobile phone reimbursement of Rs 500.
Clause 5.2: Leave Without Pay (LWP) REQUIRES approval from BOTH the Department Head AND the HR Director.
Clause 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days REQUIRES approval from the Municipal Commissioner.
Clause 6.1: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] All reimbursement claims must be submitted via the CMC employee portal using Form FIN-EXP1.
Clause 6.2: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Original receipts must be attached. Photocopies and screenshots of digital receipts are accepted only if the vendor does not issue physical receipts.
Clause 6.3: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Claims are processed within 15 working days of submission.
Clause 6.4: [NEEDS_REVIEW: VERBATIM] Disputed claims must be raised with the Finance Department within 10 working days of receiving the reimbursement decision.
