POLICY SUMMARY: policy_it_acceptable_use
============================================================

Section 2.1: Corporate devices (laptops, desktops, mobile phones issued by
  CMC) must be used primarily for official work purposes.

Section 2.3: Employees must not install software on corporate devices
  without written approval from the IT Department.

Section 3.1: Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the
  CMC employee self-service portal only.
