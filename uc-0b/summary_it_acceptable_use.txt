Policy Summary (Clause-Faithful)

[1.1] This policy governs acceptable use of all IT systems, devices, networks, and data owned or managed by the City Municipal Corporation (CMC). [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[1.2] This policy applies to all permanent employees, contractual staff, and third-party vendors with CMC system access. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[1.3] Use of CMC IT resources implies acceptance of this policy. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[2.1] (binding: must) Corporate devices (laptops, desktops, mobile phones issued by CMC) must be used primarily for official work purposes. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[2.2] Personal use of corporate devices is permitted in moderation, provided it does not interfere with work duties or consume excessive bandwidth. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[2.3] (binding: must) Employees must not install software on corporate devices without written approval from the IT Department. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[2.4] (binding: must) Software approved for installation must be sourced from the CMC-approved software catalogue only. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[2.5] (binding: must) Corporate devices must not be used to access gambling, adult content, or any site classified as harmful by the IT Department. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[2.6] (binding: must) Corporate devices must have the CMC endpoint security agent installed and active at all times. Disabling or circumventing this agent is a disciplinary offence. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[3.1] (binding: may) Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the CMC employee self-service portal only. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[3.2] (binding: must) Personal devices must not be used to access, store, or transmit classified or sensitive CMC data. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[3.3] (binding: must) Personal devices must not be connected to the CMC internal network (wired or wireless). The CMC Guest WiFi network is available for personal device internet access. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[3.4] (binding: must) Employees using personal devices for CMC email must enable device-level PIN or biometric lock. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[3.5] (binding: must) If a personal device containing CMC email is lost or stolen, the employee must report it to the IT helpdesk within 4 hours so that a remote wipe of CMC data can be performed. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[4.1] (binding: must) Employees must not share their CMC system passwords with any other person, including IT staff. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[4.2] (binding: will) IT staff will never ask for your password. Any request for your password should be reported to the IT Security team. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[4.3] (binding: must) Passwords must be changed every 90 days as prompted by the system. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[4.4] (binding: mandatory) Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all remote access to CMC systems. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[5.1] (binding: must) CMC data classified as Confidential or Restricted must not be stored on personal devices, personal cloud storage, or any system not approved by the IT Department. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[5.2] (binding: must) Employees must not forward CMC email containing Confidential data to personal email accounts. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[5.3] (binding: must) Printing of Confidential documents must use the secure print function. Documents must not be left unattended at printers. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[6.1] Internet use on CMC systems is monitored and logged. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[6.2] (binding: must) Employees must not use CMC email addresses to register for personal services, social media, or non-work subscriptions. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[6.3] (binding: must) Mass emails to external recipients must be approved by the Communications Department before sending. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[7.1] (binding: may) Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[7.2] (binding: will) Violations involving unauthorised access to restricted data will be reported to law enforcement. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
[7.3] CMC reserves the right to monitor, access, and audit any activity on CMC-owned systems at any time without prior notice. [FLAG: VERBATIM_USED]
