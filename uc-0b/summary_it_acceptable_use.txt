# Policy Document Summary

*Enforcement Rules Applied:*
- Every numbered clause is fully present.
- Multi-condition obligations are preserved exactly.
- Clauses with strict obligations are quoted verbatim to prevent meaning loss.

## Structured Clauses

**Clause 1.1 [VERBATIM]**: "This policy governs acceptable use of all IT systems, devices, networks, and data owned or managed by the City Municipal Corporation (CMC)."
**Clause 1.2 [VERBATIM]**: "This policy applies to all permanent employees, contractual staff, and third-party vendors with CMC system access."
**Clause 1.3**: Use of CMC IT resources implies acceptance of this policy.
**Clause 2.1 [VERBATIM]**: "Corporate devices (laptops, desktops, mobile phones issued by CMC) must be used primarily for official work purposes."
**Clause 2.2 [VERBATIM]**: "Personal use of corporate devices is permitted in moderation, provided it does not interfere with work duties or consume excessive bandwidth."
**Clause 2.3 [VERBATIM]**: "Employees must not install software on corporate devices without written approval from the IT Department."
**Clause 2.4 [VERBATIM]**: "Software approved for installation must be sourced from the CMC-approved software catalogue only."
**Clause 2.5 [VERBATIM]**: "Corporate devices must not be used to access gambling, adult content, or any site classified as harmful by the IT Department."
**Clause 2.6 [VERBATIM]**: "Corporate devices must have the CMC endpoint security agent installed and active at all times. Disabling or circumventing this agent is a disciplinary offence."
**Clause 3.1 [VERBATIM]**: "Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the CMC employee self-service portal only."
**Clause 3.2 [VERBATIM]**: "Personal devices must not be used to access, store, or transmit classified or sensitive CMC data."
**Clause 3.3 [VERBATIM]**: "Personal devices must not be connected to the CMC internal network (wired or wireless). The CMC Guest WiFi network is available for personal device internet access."
**Clause 3.4 [VERBATIM]**: "Employees using personal devices for CMC email must enable device-level PIN or biometric lock."
**Clause 3.5 [VERBATIM]**: "If a personal device containing CMC email is lost or stolen, the employee must report it to the IT helpdesk within 4 hours so that a remote wipe of CMC data can be performed."
**Clause 4.1 [VERBATIM]**: "Employees must not share their CMC system passwords with any other person, including IT staff."
**Clause 4.2 [VERBATIM]**: "IT staff will never ask for your password. Any request for your password should be reported to the IT Security team."
**Clause 4.3 [VERBATIM]**: "Passwords must be changed every 90 days as prompted by the system."
**Clause 4.4**: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all remote access to CMC systems.
**Clause 5.1 [VERBATIM]**: "CMC data classified as Confidential or Restricted must not be stored on personal devices, personal cloud storage, or any system not approved by the IT Department."
**Clause 5.2 [VERBATIM]**: "Employees must not forward CMC email containing Confidential data to personal email accounts."
**Clause 5.3 [VERBATIM]**: "Printing of Confidential documents must use the secure print function. Documents must not be left unattended at printers."
**Clause 6.1 [VERBATIM]**: "Internet use on CMC systems is monitored and logged."
**Clause 6.2 [VERBATIM]**: "Employees must not use CMC email addresses to register for personal services, social media, or non-work subscriptions."
**Clause 6.3 [VERBATIM]**: "Mass emails to external recipients must be approved by the Communications Department before sending."
**Clause 7.1 [VERBATIM]**: "Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment."
**Clause 7.2 [VERBATIM]**: "Violations involving unauthorised access to restricted data will be reported to law enforcement."
**Clause 7.3 [VERBATIM]**: "CMC reserves the right to monitor, access, and audit any activity on CMC-owned systems at any time without prior notice."
