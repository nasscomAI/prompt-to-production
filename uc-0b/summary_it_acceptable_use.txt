# Policy Summary: Acceptable Use Policy — IT Systems (IT-POL-003)
Status: All specific obligations and multi-condition clauses preserved.

## Key Clauses & Obligations
- Clause 2.3: Software installation requires written IT Department approval (must).
- Clause 2.5: Gambling/adult/harmful content access is prohibited on corporate devices.
- Clause 3.1: Personal devices restricted to CMC email/self-service portal ONLY.
- Clause 3.5: Lost/stolen personal device with CMC email must be reported within 4 hours for remote wipe.
- Clause 4.1: Passwords must NOT be shared even with IT staff.
- Clause 4.4: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for remote access.
- Clause 5.1: Confidential/Restricted data must NOT be stored on personal devices or cloud (must).
- Clause 6.2: CMC email addresses not for personal services/social media register.
- Clause 6.3: Mass external emails require Communications Department approval (must).
