HR LEAVE POLICY - BINDING OBLIGATIONS SUMMARY

Note: Clauses with complex conditions are quoted verbatim to prevent meaning loss.

- Clause 2.1: "Corporate devices (laptops, desktops, mobile phones issued by CMC) must be used primarily for official work purposes."
- Clause 2.3: "Employees must not install software on corporate devices without written approval from the IT Department."
- Clause 2.4: "Software approved for installation must be sourced from the CMC-approved software catalogue only."
- Clause 2.5: "Corporate devices must not be used to access gambling, adult content, or any site classified as harmful by the IT Department."
- Clause 2.6: "Corporate devices must have the CMC endpoint security agent installed and active at all times. Disabling or circumventing this agent is a disciplinary offence."
- Clause 3.2: "Personal devices must not be used to access, store, or transmit classified or sensitive CMC data."
- Clause 3.3: "Personal devices must not be connected to the CMC internal network (wired or wireless). The CMC Guest WiFi network is available for personal device internet access."
- Clause 3.4: "Employees using personal devices for CMC email must enable device-level PIN or biometric lock."
- Clause 3.5: "If a personal device containing CMC email is lost or stolen, the employee must report it to the IT helpdesk within 4 hours so that a remote wipe of CMC data can be performed."
- Clause 4.1: "Employees must not share their CMC system passwords with any other person, including IT staff."
- Clause 4.2: "IT staff will never ask for your password. Any request for your password should be reported to the IT Security team."
- Clause 4.3: "Passwords must be changed every 90 days as prompted by the system."
- Clause 5.1: "CMC data classified as Confidential or Restricted must not be stored on personal devices, personal cloud storage, or any system not approved by the IT Department."
- Clause 5.2: "Employees must not forward CMC email containing Confidential data to personal email accounts."
- Clause 5.3: "Printing of Confidential documents must use the secure print function. Documents must not be left unattended at printers."
- Clause 6.2: "Employees must not use CMC email addresses to register for personal services, social media, or non-work subscriptions."
- Clause 6.3: "Mass emails to external recipients must be approved by the Communications Department before sending."
- Clause 7.2: "Violations involving unauthorised access to restricted data will be reported to law enforcement."
