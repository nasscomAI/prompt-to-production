# HR Leave Policy Summary

- **Clause 1.1**: This policy governs acceptable use of all IT systems, devices, networks, and data owned or managed by the City Municipal Corporation (CMC).
- **Clause 1.2**: This policy applies to all permanent employees, contractual staff, and third-party vendors with CMC system access.
- **Clause 1.3**: Use of CMC IT resources implies acceptance of this policy. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 2. CORPORATE DEVICES ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
- **Clause 2.1**: Corporate devices (laptops, desktops, mobile phones issued by CMC) must be used primarily for official work purposes.
- **Clause 2.2**: Personal use of corporate devices is permitted in moderation, provided it does not interfere with work duties or consume excessive bandwidth.
- **Clause 2.3 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Employees must not install software on corporate devices without written approval from the IT Department.
- **Clause 2.4 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Software approved for installation must be sourced from the CMC-approved software catalogue only.
- **Clause 2.5 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Corporate devices must not be used to access gambling, adult content, or any site classified as harmful by the IT Department.
- **Clause 2.6 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Corporate devices must have the CMC endpoint security agent installed and active at all times. Disabling or circumventing this agent is a disciplinary offence. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 3. PERSONAL DEVICES (BYOD) ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
- **Clause 3.1**: Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the CMC employee self-service portal only.
- **Clause 3.2 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Personal devices must not be used to access, store, or transmit classified or sensitive CMC data.
- **Clause 3.3**: Personal devices must not be connected to the CMC internal network (wired or wireless). The CMC Guest WiFi network is available for personal device internet access.
- **Clause 3.4 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Employees using personal devices for CMC email must enable device-level PIN or biometric lock.
- **Clause 3.5**: If a personal device containing CMC email is lost or stolen, the employee must report it to the IT helpdesk within 4 hours so that a remote wipe of CMC data can be performed. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 4. PASSWORDS AND ACCESS CONTROL ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
- **Clause 4.1**: Employees must not share their CMC system passwords with any other person, including IT staff.
- **Clause 4.2**: IT staff will never ask for your password. Any request for your password should be reported to the IT Security team.
- **Clause 4.3**: Passwords must be changed every 90 days as prompted by the system.
- **Clause 4.4**: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all remote access to CMC systems. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 5. DATA HANDLING ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
- **Clause 5.1**: CMC data classified as Confidential or Restricted must not be stored on personal devices, personal cloud storage, or any system not approved by the IT Department.
- **Clause 5.2 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Employees must not forward CMC email containing Confidential data to personal email accounts.
- **Clause 5.3 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Printing of Confidential documents must use the secure print function. Documents must not be left unattended at printers. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 6. INTERNET AND EMAIL USE ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
- **Clause 6.1**: Internet use on CMC systems is monitored and logged.
- **Clause 6.2**: Employees must not use CMC email addresses to register for personal services, social media, or non-work subscriptions.
- **Clause 6.3**: Mass emails to external recipients must be approved by the Communications Department before sending. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 7. VIOLATIONS AND CONSEQUENCES ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
- **Clause 7.1**: Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.
- **Clause 7.2 [FLAGGED VERBATIM OUT OF CAUTION]**: Violations involving unauthorised access to restricted data will be reported to law enforcement.
- **Clause 7.3**: CMC reserves the right to monitor, access, and audit any activity on CMC-owned systems at any time without prior notice.
