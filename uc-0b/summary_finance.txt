Clause 1.1: This policy governs reimbursement of expenses incurred by CMC employees in the course of official duties.
Clause 1.2: Personal expenses are not reimbursable under any circumstances.
Clause 1.3: All claims must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the expense being incurred.
Clause 2.1: Receipts are required for all claims above Rs 200.
Clause 2.2: Outstation travel must be pre-approved using Form FIN-T1 before travel commences.
Clause 2.3: Economy class is mandatory. [COMPLEX]
Clause 2.4: Hotel accommodation for outstation travel is reimbursable up to Rs 3,500 per night for Grade A cities and Rs 2,500 per night for other locations. [COMPLEX]
Clause 2.5: No separate meal receipts are required if DA is claimed.
Clause 2.6: If actual meal expenses are claimed instead of DA, receipts are mandatory and the combined meal claim must not exceed Rs 750 per day. [COMPLEX]
Clause 3.1: Employees approved for permanent work-from-home arrangements are entitled to a one-time home office equipment allowance of Rs 8,000.
Clause 3.2: The allowance covers: desk, chair, monitor, keyboard, mouse, and networking equipment only. [COMPLEX]
Clause 3.3: The allowance does not cover: personal computers, laptops, smartphones, printers, or air conditioning equipment.
Clause 3.4: Claims must be submitted with original receipts within 60 days of the work-from-home arrangement being approved in writing by the Department Head.
Clause 3.5: Employees on temporary or partial work-from-home arrangements are not eligible for this allowance.
Clause 4.1: Training expenses are reimbursable only if the training was pre-approved by the Department Head using Form FIN-TR1.
Clause 4.2: Course fees are reimbursable up to Rs 15,000 per financial year per employee.
Clause 4.3: Exam fees for professional certifications are reimbursable once per attempt per certification, up to Rs 5,000.
Clause 4.4: If an employee leaves CMC within 12 months of a reimbursed training expense, they must repay 100% of the reimbursed amount.
Clause 5.1: Employees in Grade C and above are entitled to a monthly mobile phone reimbursement of Rs 500. [COMPLEX]
Clause 5.2: Employees in Grade B and above are entitled to a monthly internet reimbursement of Rs 800 for approved work-from-home arrangements only. [COMPLEX]
Clause 5.3: Reimbursements under this section require submission of the original bill each month.
Clause 6.1: All reimbursement claims must be submitted via the CMC employee portal using Form FIN-EXP1.
Clause 6.2: Original receipts must be attached.
Clause 6.3: Claims are processed within 15 working days of submission.
Clause 6.4: Disputed claims must be raised with the Finance Department within 10 working days of receiving the reimbursement decision.
