HIGH-FIDELITY CLAUSE SUMMARY:

Clause 1.3: All claims must be submitted within 30 calendar days; late claims will not be processed.
Clause 2.2: Outstation travel must be pre-approved; travel without prior approval is not reimbursable.
Clause 2.6: DA and actual meal receipts cannot be claimed simultaneously for the same day.
Clause 3.4: WFH equipment claims must be submitted with original receipts within 60 days and approved in writing by the Dept Head.
Clause 4.4: Employees leaving CMC within 12 months must repay 100% of training costs; between 12-24 months, 50% must be repaid.
Clause 6.1: All claims must be submitted via the employee portal using Form FIN-EXP1.
Clause 6.4: Disputed claims must be raised with the Finance Department within 10 working days.
