POLICY SUMMARY WITH CLAUSE ENFORCEMENT

============================================================

NOTE: Each clause reference below maps to the source document.


### SECTION 1


[Clause 1.1]
Requirement: This policy governs all leave entitlements for permanent and contractual employees of the City Municipal Corporation (CMC).
Binding verb: None

[Clause 1.2]
Requirement: This policy does not apply to daily wage workers or consultants. Those categories are governed by their respective contracts.
Binding verb: None

### SECTION 2


[Clause 2.1]
Requirement: Each permanent employee is entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Binding verb: None

[Clause 2.2]
Requirement: Annual leave accrues at 1.5 days per month from the date of joining.
Binding verb: None

[Clause 2.3]
Requirement: Employees must submit a leave application at least 14 calendar days in advance using Form HR-L1.
Binding verb: must

[Clause 2.4]
Requirement: Leave applications must receive written approval from the employee's direct manager before the leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid.
Binding verb: must

[Clause 2.5]
Requirement: Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
Binding verb: will

[Clause 2.6]
Requirement: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December.
Binding verb: may

[Clause 2.7]
Requirement: Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter (January–March) of the following year or they are forfeited.
Binding verb: must

### SECTION 3


[Clause 3.1]
Requirement: Each employee is entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per calendar year.
Binding verb: None

[Clause 3.2]
Requirement: Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner, submitted within 48 hours of returning to work.
Binding verb: requires

[Clause 3.3]
Requirement: Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the following year.
Binding verb: cannot

[Clause 3.4]
Requirement: Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday or annual leave period requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.
Reason: Stricter medical cert requirement for leave adjacent to holidays.
Binding verb: requires

### SECTION 4


[Clause 4.1]
Requirement: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for the first two live births.
Binding verb: None

[Clause 4.2]
Requirement: For a third or subsequent child, maternity leave is 12 weeks paid.
Binding verb: None

[Clause 4.3]
Requirement: Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paid paternity leave, to be taken within 30 days of the child's birth.
Binding verb: None

[Clause 4.4]
Requirement: Paternity leave cannot be split across multiple periods.
Binding verb: cannot

### SECTION 5


[Clause 5.1]
Requirement: An employee may apply for Leave Without Pay only after exhausting all applicable paid leave entitlements.
Binding verb: may

[Clause 5.2]
Requirement: LWP requires approval from BOTH the Department Head AND the HR Director.
Reason: Multi-condition obligation — manager approval alone is insufficient.
Binding verb: requires

[Clause 5.3]
Requirement: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
Reason: Additional escalation condition for extended LWP.
Binding verb: requires

[Clause 5.4]
Requirement: Periods of LWP do not count toward service for the purposes of seniority, increments, or retirement benefits.
Binding verb: None

### SECTION 6


[Clause 6.1]
Requirement: Employees are entitled to all gazetted public holidays as declared by the State Government each year.
Binding verb: None

[Clause 6.2]
Requirement: If an employee is required to work on a public holiday, they are entitled to one compensatory off day, to be taken within 60 days of the holiday worked.
Binding verb: None

[Clause 6.3]
Requirement: Compensatory off cannot be encashed.
Binding verb: cannot

### SECTION 7


[Clause 7.1]
Requirement: Annual leave may be encashed only at the time of retirement or resignation, subject to a maximum of 60 days.
Binding verb: may

[Clause 7.2]
Requirement: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
Binding verb: not permitted

[Clause 7.3]
Requirement: Sick leave and LWP cannot be encashed under any circumstances.
Binding verb: cannot

### SECTION 8


[Clause 8.1]
Requirement: Leave-related grievances must be raised with the HR Department within 10 working days of the disputed decision.
Binding verb: must

[Clause 8.2]
Requirement: Grievances raised after 10 working days will not be considered unless exceptional circumstances are demonstrated in writing.
Binding verb: will
