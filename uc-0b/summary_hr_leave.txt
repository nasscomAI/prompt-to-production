HR LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY (UC-0B COMPLIANT)
------------------------------------------
Clause 2.3: 14-day advance notice required (must).
Clause 2.4: Written approval required before leave; verbal is not valid (must).
Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence results in LOP regardless of later approval (will).
Clause 2.6: Max 5 days carry-forward; excess forfeited on 31 Dec (may/forfeited).
Clause 2.7: Carry-forward must be used Jan–Mar or forfeited (must).
Clause 3.2: 3+ consecutive sick days requires medical cert within 48hrs (requires).
Clause 3.4: Sick leave near holidays requires cert regardless of duration (requires).
Clause 5.2 [STRICT]: LWP requires BOTH Department Head AND HR Director approval.
Clause 5.3: LWP >30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval (requires).
Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during service is NOT permitted under any circumstances.
