CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATION
HUMAN RESOURCES DEPARTMENT
EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY
Document Reference: HR-POL-001
Version: 2.3 | Effective: 1 April 2024
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 1: 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 2: 1.1 This policy governs all leave entitlements for permanent and
contractual employees of the City Municipal Corporation (CMC).
Clause 3: 1.2 This policy does not apply to daily wage workers or consultants.
Those categories are governed by their respective contracts.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 4: 2. ANNUAL LEAVE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 5: 2.1 Each permanent employee is entitled to 18 days of paid annual
leave per calendar year.
Clause 6: 2.2 Annual leave accrues at 1.5 days per month from the date of
joining.
Clause 7: 2.3 Employees must submit a leave application at least 14 calendar
days in advance using Form HR-L1.
Clause 8: 2.4 Leave applications must receive written approval from the
employee's direct manager before the leave commences.
Verbal approval is not valid.
Clause 9: 2.5 Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP)
regardless of subsequent approval.
Clause 10: 2.6 Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave
days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are
forfeited on 31 December.
Clause 11: 2.7 Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter
(January–March) of the following year or they are forfeited.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 12: 3. SICK LEAVE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 13: 3.1 Each employee is entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per
calendar year.
Clause 14: 3.2 Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical
certificate from a registered medical practitioner, submitted
within 48 hours of returning to work.
Clause 15: 3.3 Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the following year.
Clause 16: 3.4 Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday
or annual leave period requires a medical certificate regardless
of duration.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 17: 4. MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 18: 4.1 Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity
leave for the first two live births.
Clause 19: 4.2 For a third or subsequent child, maternity leave is 12 weeks
paid.
Clause 20: 4.3 Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paid paternity leave,
to be taken within 30 days of the child's birth.
Clause 21: 4.4 Paternity leave cannot be split across multiple periods.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 22: 5. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 23: 5.1 An employee may apply for Leave Without Pay only after
exhausting all applicable paid leave entitlements.
Clause 24: 5.2 LWP requires approval from the Department Head and the
HR Director. Manager approval alone is not sufficient.
Clause 25: 5.3 LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from
the Municipal Commissioner.
Clause 26: 5.4 Periods of LWP do not count toward service for the purposes
of seniority, increments, or retirement benefits.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 27: 6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 28: 6.1 Employees are entitled to all gazetted public holidays as
declared by the State Government each year.
Clause 29: 6.2 If an employee is required to work on a public holiday, they
are entitled to one compensatory off day, to be taken within
Clause 30: 60 days of the holiday worked.
Clause 31: 6.3 Compensatory off cannot be encashed.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 32: 7. LEAVE ENCASHMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 33: 7.1 Annual leave may be encashed only at the time of retirement
or resignation, subject to a maximum of 60 days.
Clause 34: 7.2 Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any
circumstances.
Clause 35: 7.3 Sick leave and LWP cannot be encashed under any circumstances.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 36: 8. GRIEVANCES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 37: 8.1 Leave-related grievances must be raised with the HR Department
within 10 working days of the disputed decision.
Clause 38: 8.2 Grievances raised after 10 working days will not be considered
unless exceptional circumstances are demonstrated in writing.
