# Employee Leave Policy Summary

## Purpose and Scope
Governs entitlements for permanent and contractual employees.

## Key Clauses and Obligations
- **Clause 2.3**: 14-day advance notice required (must)
- **Clause 2.4**: Written approval required before leave commences. Verbal not valid. (must)
- **Clause 2.5**: Unapproved absence = LOP regardless of subsequent approval (will)
- **Clause 2.6**: Max 5 days carry-forward. Above 5 forfeited on 31 Dec. (may / are forfeited)
- **Clause 2.7**: Carry-forward days must be used Jan–Mar or forfeited (must)
- **Clause 3.2**: 3+ consecutive sick days requires medical cert within 48hrs (requires)
- **Clause 3.4**: Sick leave before/after holiday requires cert regardless of duration (requires)
- **Clause 5.2**: LWP requires approval from both Department Head AND HR Director (requires)
- **Clause 5.3**: LWP > 30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval (requires)
- **Clause 7.2**: Leave encashment during service not permitted under any circumstances (not permitted)

## Enforcements and Refusals
- All approvals must be written; verbal approval is strictly not valid.
- Unapproved absences are recorded as LOP without exception.
- Carry-forward rules for annual leave are strictly bound to the first quarter.
