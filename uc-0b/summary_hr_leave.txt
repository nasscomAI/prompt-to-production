=== EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY ===

This policy does not apply to daily wage workers or consultants. [1.2]
Each permanent employee is entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. [2.1]
Annual leave accrues at 1. [2.2]
Leave applications must be submitted 14 calendar days in advance via Form HR-L1. [2.3]
Prior written approval from the direct manager is mandatory; verbal approval is explicitly not valid. [2.4]
Unapproved absence will result in Loss of Pay (LOP), even if approved later. [2.5]
A maximum of 5 annual leave days can be carried forward; any excess is forfeited on 31 December. [2.6]
Carry-forward days must be used between January and March, or they will be forfeited. [2.7]
Each employee is entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per calendar year. [3.1]
Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate submitted within 48 hours of return. [3.2]
Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the following year. [3.3]
Sick leave taken adjacent to public holidays or annual leave requires a medical certificate regardless of duration. [3.4]
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for the first two live births. [4.1]
For a third or subsequent child, maternity leave is 12 weeks paid. [4.2]
