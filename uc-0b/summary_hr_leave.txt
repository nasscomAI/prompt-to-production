HR LEAVE POLICY - STRICT SUMMARY
========================================

Clause 1.1: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: This policy governs all leave entitlements for permanent and contractual employees of the City Municipal Corporation (CMC).
Clause 1.2: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: This policy does not apply to daily wage workers or consultants. Those categories are governed by their respective contracts. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 2. ANNUAL LEAVE ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 2.1: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Each permanent employee is entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Clause 2.2: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Annual leave accrues at 1.5 days per month from the date of joining.
Clause 2.3: A 14-day advance notice is required.
Clause 2.4: Leave must have written approval before it commences; verbal approvals are invalid.
Clause 2.5: Any unapproved absence will result in LOP, regardless of if it is subsequently approved.
Clause 2.6: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 days; days exceeding this are forfeited on 31 Dec.
Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used between Jan-Mar or they will be forfeited.
Clause 3.1: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Each employee is entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per calendar year.
Clause 3.2: Taking 3 or more consecutive sick days requires a medical certificate within 48 hours.
Clause 3.3: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the following year.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave taken immediately before or after a holiday requires a medical certificate regardless of the duration.
Clause 4.1: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for the first two live births.
Clause 4.2: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: For a third or subsequent child, maternity leave is 12 weeks paid.
Clause 4.3: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paid paternity leave, to be taken within 30 days of the child's birth.
Clause 4.4: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Paternity leave cannot be split across multiple periods. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 5. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP) ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 5.1: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: An employee may apply for Leave Without Pay only after exhausting all applicable paid leave entitlements.
Clause 5.2: LWP requires approval from both the Department Head AND the HR Director.
Clause 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
Clause 5.4: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Periods of LWP do not count toward service for the purposes of seniority, increments, or retirement benefits. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 6.1: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Employees are entitled to all gazetted public holidays as declared by the State Government each year.
Clause 6.2: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: If an employee is required to work on a public holiday, they are entitled to one compensatory off day, to be taken within 60 days of the holiday worked.
Clause 6.3: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Compensatory off cannot be encashed. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 7. LEAVE ENCASHMENT ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 7.1: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Annual leave may be encashed only at the time of retirement or resignation, subject to a maximum of 60 days.
Clause 7.2: Leave encashment is not permitted under any circumstances during active service.
Clause 7.3: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Sick leave and LWP cannot be encashed under any circumstances. ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════ 8. GRIEVANCES ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Clause 8.1: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Leave-related grievances must be raised with the HR Department within 10 working days of the disputed decision.
Clause 8.2: [Verbatim Quote due to complexity]: Grievances raised after 10 working days will not be considered unless exceptional circumstances are demonstrated in writing.
