HR LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY

=======================

This summary preserves critical binding obligations without dropping any conditions.


Clause 2.3 (Verbatim Quote): Employees must submit a leave application at least 14 calendar days in advance using Form HR-L1.

Clause 2.4 (Verbatim Quote): Leave applications must receive written approval from the employee's direct manager before the leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid.

Clause 2.5 (Verbatim Quote): Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.

Clause 2.6 (Verbatim Quote): Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December.

Clause 2.7 (Verbatim Quote): Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter (January–March) of the following year or they are forfeited.

Clause 3.2 (Verbatim Quote): Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner, submitted within 48 hours of returning to work.

Clause 3.4 (Verbatim Quote): Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday or annual leave period requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.

Clause 5.2 (Verbatim Quote): LWP requires approval from the Department Head and the HR Director. Manager approval alone is not sufficient.

Clause 5.3 (Verbatim Quote): LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.

Clause 7.2 (Verbatim Quote): Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
