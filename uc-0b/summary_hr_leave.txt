1.1 Policy applies to permanent and contractual employees of CMC.
1.2 Policy does not apply to daily wage workers or consultants.

2.1 Employees are entitled to 18 days annual leave per year.
2.2 Leave accrues at 1.5 days per month.
2.3 Leave application must be submitted at least 14 days in advance.
2.4 Leave must receive written approval before commencement; verbal approval is not valid.
2.5 Unapproved absence will be treated as Loss of Pay regardless of later approval.
2.6 Maximum 5 days can be carried forward; excess days are forfeited on 31 December.
2.7 Carry-forward leave must be used between January and March or it is forfeited.

3.1 Employees are entitled to 12 days sick leave per year.
3.2 Sick leave of 3 or more days requires a medical certificate within 48 hours of return.
3.3 Sick leave cannot be carried forward.
3.4 Sick leave before or after holidays requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.

4.1 Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks maternity leave for first two births.
4.2 Third or subsequent maternity leave is 12 weeks.
4.3 Male employees are entitled to 5 days paternity leave within 30 days of birth.
4.4 Paternity leave cannot be split.

5.1 Leave Without Pay can be applied only after exhausting paid leave.
5.2 LWP requires approval from BOTH Department Head and HR Director; manager approval alone is not sufficient.
5.3 LWP exceeding 30 days requires approval from Municipal Commissioner.
5.4 LWP period does not count for service benefits.

6.1 Employees are entitled to declared public holidays.
6.2 Working on a holiday grants one compensatory off within 60 days.
6.3 Compensatory off cannot be encashed.

7.1 Annual leave encashment allowed only at resignation or retirement up to 60 days.
7.2 Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
7.3 Sick leave and LWP cannot be encashed.

8.1 Grievances must be raised within 10 working days.
8.2 Late grievances are not considered unless exceptional circumstances are provided.
