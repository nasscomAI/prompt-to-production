HR LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY
==============================
[SUMMARY] 2.3 Employees must provide a 14-day advance notice for planned leave.
[VERBATIM] 2.4 Written approval requires signature before leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid.
[VERBATIM] 2.5 Unapproved absence will result in Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
[VERBATIM] 2.6 A maximum of 5 days may be carried forward. Days above 5 are forfeited on 31 Dec.
[VERBATIM] 2.7 Carry-forward days must be used between Jan–Mar or they are forfeited.
[VERBATIM] 3.2 3 or more consecutive sick days requires a medical certificate within 48hrs.
[VERBATIM] 3.4 Sick leave immediately before or after a holiday requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.
[VERBATIM] 5.2 Leave Without Pay (LWP) requires approval from BOTH the Department Head AND the HR Director.
[VERBATIM] 5.3 LWP exceeding 30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
[VERBATIM] 7.2 Leave encashment during active service is not permitted under any circumstances.
