HR LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY (STRICT ENFORCEMENT)
============================================
Clause 2.3: 14-day advance notice required (must) using Form HR-L1.
Clause 2.4: Written approval required before leave commences. Verbal not valid. (must)
Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence = LOP regardless of subsequent approval. (will)
Clause 2.6: Max 5 days may be carried forward; any above 5 are forfeited on 31 Dec.
Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used January-March or forfeited.
Clause 3.2: Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires medical cert within 48 hours.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave immediately before/after a holiday requires cert regardless of duration.
Clause 5.2: LWP requires approval from BOTH Department Head AND HR Director.
Clause 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.

Note: No external practices have been added. All multi-condition obligations have been retained.
