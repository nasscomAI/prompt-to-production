CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATION - POLICY SUMMARY
============================================
CLAUSE 2.3: 14-day advance notice required via Form HR-L1.
CLAUSE 2.4: Mandatory written approval from direct manager before leave; verbal approval is strictly invalid.
CLAUSE 2.5: Unapproved absence results in Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
CLAUSE 2.6: Maximum 5 days carry-forward allowed; any excess is forfeited on 31 December.
CLAUSE 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used by 31 March (Q1) or they are forfeited.
CLAUSE 3.2: Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires medical certificate submission within 48 hours of return.
CLAUSE 3.4: Medical certificate required for sick leave adjacent to holidays/annual leave, regardless of duration.
CLAUSE 5.2: LWP requires approval from BOTH Department Head AND HR Director. Manager approval is insufficient.
CLAUSE 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
CLAUSE 7.2: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
