CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATION — HR LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY
Document Reference: HR-POL-001 | Version: 2.3 | Effective: 1 April 2024

This summary preserves all binding obligations from the source document.
No information has been added beyond what appears in the policy.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. SCOPE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1.1 Applies to all permanent and contractual employees of CMC.
1.2 Does NOT apply to daily wage workers or consultants — governed by their contracts.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. ANNUAL LEAVE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2.1 Entitlement: 18 days paid annual leave per calendar year.
2.2 Accrual: 1.5 days per month from date of joining.
2.3 [OBLIGATION] Employees MUST submit leave application at least 14 calendar days in advance using Form HR-L1.
2.4 [OBLIGATION] Leave MUST receive written approval from direct manager before leave commences. Verbal approval is NOT valid.
2.5 [OBLIGATION] Unapproved absence WILL be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
2.6 [OBLIGATION] Maximum 5 unused days may be carried forward. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December.
2.7 [OBLIGATION] Carry-forward days MUST be used within January–March of the following year or they are forfeited.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
3. SICK LEAVE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
3.1 Entitlement: 12 days paid sick leave per calendar year.
3.2 [OBLIGATION] Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days REQUIRES medical certificate from registered practitioner, submitted within 48 hours of returning to work.
3.3 Sick leave cannot be carried forward.
3.4 [OBLIGATION] Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday or annual leave period REQUIRES medical certificate regardless of duration.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4. MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4.1 Maternity: 26 weeks paid for first two live births.
4.2 Maternity: 12 weeks paid for third or subsequent child.
4.3 Paternity: 5 days paid, taken within 30 days of child's birth.
4.4 Paternity leave cannot be split across multiple periods.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5.1 LWP may only be applied for after exhausting all paid leave entitlements.
5.2 [OBLIGATION — MULTI-CONDITION] LWP REQUIRES approval from BOTH the Department Head AND the HR Director. Manager approval alone is NOT sufficient.
5.3 [OBLIGATION] LWP exceeding 30 continuous days REQUIRES approval from the Municipal Commissioner.
5.4 LWP periods do not count toward seniority, increments, or retirement benefits.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
6.1 Entitled to all gazetted public holidays declared by State Government.
6.2 Working on a public holiday entitles employee to one compensatory off day, taken within 60 days.
6.3 Compensatory off cannot be encashed.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
7. LEAVE ENCASHMENT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
7.1 Annual leave may be encashed only at retirement or resignation, maximum 60 days.
7.2 [VERBATIM — OBLIGATION] "Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances."
7.3 Sick leave and LWP cannot be encashed under any circumstances.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
8. GRIEVANCES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
8.1 Leave grievances must be raised with HR Department within 10 working days of disputed decision.
8.2 Grievances after 10 working days will not be considered unless exceptional circumstances are demonstrated in writing.
