HR LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY
==================================================

Clause 2.3:
  Obligation: 14-day advance notice required
  Binding: must
  Source: Employees must submit a leave application at least 14 calendar days in advance using Form HR-L1.

Clause 2.4:
  Obligation: Written approval required before leave commences. Verbal not valid.
  Binding: must
  Source: Leave applications must receive written approval from the employee's direct manager before the leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid.

Clause 2.5:
  Obligation: Unapproved absence = LOP regardless of subsequent approval
  Binding: will
  Source: Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.

Clause 2.6:
  Obligation: Max 5 days carry-forward. Above 5 forfeited on 31 Dec.
  Binding: may / are forfeited
  Source: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December.

Clause 2.7:
  Obligation: Carry-forward days must be used Jan–Mar or forfeited
  Binding: must
  Source: Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter (January–March) of the following year or they are forfeited.

Clause 3.2:
  Obligation: 3+ consecutive sick days requires medical cert within 48hrs
  Binding: requires
  Source: Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner, submitted within 48 hours of returning to work.

Clause 3.4:
  Obligation: Sick leave before/after holiday requires cert regardless of duration
  Binding: requires
  Source: Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday or annual leave period requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.

Clause 5.2:
  Obligation: LWP requires Department Head AND HR Director approval
  Binding: requires
  Source: LWP requires approval from the Department Head and the HR Director. Manager approval alone is not sufficient.

Clause 5.3:
  Obligation: LWP >30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval
  Binding: requires
  Source: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.

Clause 7.2:
  Obligation: Leave encashment during service not permitted under any circumstances
  Binding: not permitted
  Source: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
