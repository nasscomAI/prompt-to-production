HR Leave Policy Summary:
1. Clause 2.3: 14-day advance notice must be provided.
2. Clause 2.4: Written approval must be obtained before leave commences. Verbal is not valid.
3. Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence will result in LOP regardless of subsequent approval.
4. Clause 2.6: Max 5 days carry-forward. Above 5 are forfeited on 31 Dec.
5. Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used Jan-Mar or forfeited.
6. Clause 3.2: 3+ consecutive sick days requires medical cert within 48hrs.
7. Clause 3.4: Sick leave before/after holiday requires cert regardless of duration.
8. Clause 5.2: LWP requires Department Head AND HR Director approval.
9. Clause 5.3: LWP >30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
10. Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
