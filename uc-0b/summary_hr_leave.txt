Clause 2.3: Employees must provide at least 14-day advance notice before taking leave.

Clause 2.4: Leave must be approved in writing before commencement. Verbal approval is not valid.

Clause 2.5: Any unapproved absence will be treated as Loss of Pay (LOP), regardless of later approval.

Clause 2.6: A maximum of 5 days of leave may be carried forward. Any balance above 5 days is forfeited on 31 December.

Clause 2.7: Carried-forward leave must be used between January and March, otherwise it will be forfeited.

Clause 3.2: Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate submitted within 48 hours.

Clause 3.4: Sick leave taken before or after a holiday requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.

Clause 5.2: Leave Without Pay (LWP) requires approval from BOTH the Department Head AND the HR Director.

Clause 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.

Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
