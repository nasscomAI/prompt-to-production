# Policy Summary: Leave Entitlements
This is a strict summary of the provided text. No external knowledge has been added.

## Core Obligations
- **Clause 2.3**: Employees must submit a leave application at least 14 days in advance.
- **Clause 2.4**: Employees must receive written approval before leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid.
- **Clause 2.5**: Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
- **Clause 2.6**: Employees may carry forward a max of 5 days. Days above 5 are forfeited on 31 Dec.
- **Clause 2.7**: Carry-forward days must be used in Jan-Mar or they are forfeited.
- **Clause 3.2**: 3+ consecutive sick days requires a medical certificate within 48 hours of return.
- **Clause 3.4**: Sick leave immediately before/after a holiday requires a cert regardless of duration.
- **Clause 5.2**: LWP requires approval from BOTH the Department Head AND the HR Director.
- **Clause 5.3**: LWP >30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
- **Clause 7.2**: [VERBATIM FLAG] "Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances."
