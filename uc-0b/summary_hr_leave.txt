HR LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY (STRICT COMPLIANCE)
===========================================
Clause 2.3: Application must be submitted at least 14 days in advance.
Clause 2.4: Written approval must occur before leave commences; verbal is invalid.
Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence will equal LOP regardless of later approval.
Clause 2.6: Employees may carry forward a max of 5 unused annual leave days; any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 Dec.
Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used in Jan-Mar or they are forfeited.
Clause 3.2: Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires a medical certificate within 48 hours.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave bounding a holiday requires a certificate regardless of duration.
Clause 5.2 [VERBATIM FLAG]: LWP requires approval from the Department Head and the HR Director. Manager approval alone is not sufficient.
Clause 5.3: LWP >30 continuous days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
