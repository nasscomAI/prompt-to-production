HR POLICY SUMMARY - LEAVE OBLIGATIONS
========================================

- [Clause 2.3] 14-day advance notice is mandatory for annual leave applications.
- [Clause 2.4] Written approval must be obtained before leave starts; verbal approval is not valid.
- [Clause 2.5] Unapproved absences will result in Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of any later approval.
- [Clause 2.6] Maximum 5 days carry-forward allowed; any excess is forfeited on 31 December.
- [Clause 2.7] Carry-forward days must be used between January and March or they will be forfeited.
- [Clause 3.2] Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires a medical certificate within 48 hours of return.
- [Clause 3.4] Medical certificate is required for sick leave taken immediately before/after holidays/annual leave, regardless of duration.
- [Clause 5.2] LWP requires approval from BOTH the Department Head and the HR Director.
- [Clause 5.3] LWP exceeding 30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
- [Clause 7.2] Leave encashment during service is strictly prohibited under any circumstances.

NOTE: This summary is derived strictly from the policy text. No external standards applied.
