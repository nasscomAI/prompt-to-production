1. Clause 2.3: Employees must submit a leave application at least 14 calendar days in advance using Form HR-L1.
2. Clause 2.4: Leave applications must receive written approval from the direct manager before leave commences; verbal approval is not valid.
3. Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of any subsequent approval.
4. Clause 2.6: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following year; any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December.
5. Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter (January–March) of the following year or they are forfeited.
6. Clause 3.2: Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner, submitted within 48 hours of return to work.
7. Clause 3.4: Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday or annual leave requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.
8. Clause 5.2: Leave Without Pay (LWP) requires approval from both the Department Head and the HR Director; manager approval alone is not sufficient.
9. Clause 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.
10. Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
