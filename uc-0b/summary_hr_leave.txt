CMC Employee Leave Policy Summary (Reference: HR-POL-001)
==========================================================

Clause 2.3: Employees must submit leave applications at least 14 calendar days in advance using Form HR-L1.
Clause 2.4: Written approval from the direct manager is required before leave commences; verbal approval is not valid.
Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
Clause 2.6: A maximum of 5 unused annual leave days can be carried forward; any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December.
Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter (January–March) or they are forfeited.
Clause 3.2: Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires a medical certificate submitted within 48 hours of returning.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave taken immediately before or after public holidays or annual leave requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.
Clause 5.2: Leave Without Pay (LWP) requires approval from BOTH the Department Head and the HR Director; manager approval alone is insufficient.
Clause 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.
Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.

[VERBATIM] Clause 5.2: LWP requires approval from the Department Head and the HR Director. Manager approval alone is not sufficient.
