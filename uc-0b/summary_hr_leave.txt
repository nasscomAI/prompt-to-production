City Municipal Corporation - Leave Policy Summary

Annual Leave:
- (Clause 2.3) A leave application must be submitted at least 14 calendar days in advance via Form HR-L1.
- (Clause 2.4) Written approval from the direct manager is mandatory before leave begins; verbal approval is strictly invalid.
- (Clause 2.5) Any unapproved absence is recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP), even if approved later.
- (Clause 2.6) A maximum of 5 unused annual leave days may be carried forward; any excess is forfeited on 31 December.
- (Clause 2.7) Carried-forward days must be used between January and March of the following year, or they will be forfeited.

Sick Leave:
- (Clause 3.2) For 3 or more consecutive sick days, a medical certificate from a registered practitioner must be submitted within 48 hours of return.
- (Clause 3.4) A medical certificate is required for any sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday or annual leave, regardless of the leave duration.

Leave Without Pay (LWP):
- (Clause 5.2) LWP requires dual approval from both the Department Head AND the HR Director. Manager approval alone is insufficient.
- (Clause 5.3) LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.

Leave Encashment:
- (Clause 7.2) Encashment of leave while in service is not permitted under any circumstances.
