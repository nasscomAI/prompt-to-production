HR Leave Policy Summary - Essential Binding Clauses:

1. Clause 2.3: A 14-day advance notice must be provided for leave requests.
2. Clause 2.4: Written approval must be secured before leave commences; verbal approval is not valid.
3. Clause 2.5: Any unapproved absence will result in Loss of Pay (LOP), regardless of subsequent approval.
4. Clause 2.6: A maximum of 5 days may be carried forward. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 Dec.
5. Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be utilized between January and March, otherwise they are forfeited.
6. Clause 3.2: 3 or more consecutive sick days requires a medical certificate within 48 hours.
7. Clause 3.4: Sick leave taken immediately before or after a holiday requires a medical certificate regardless of the leave duration.
8. Clause 5.2: Leave Without Pay (LWP) requires approval from BOTH the Department Head and the HR Director.
9. Clause 5.3: Leave Without Pay (LWP) exceeding 30 days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.
10. Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during active service is not permitted under any circumstances.
