HR Leave Policy Summary (Strict Compliance):

Clause 1.1: Policy applies to permanent and contractual employees of CMC.
Clause 1.2: Daily wage/consultants entirely excluded.
Clause 2.1: Permanent employees get 18 paid annual leave days per year.
Clause 2.2: Annual leave accrues 1.5 days/month from joining.
Clause 2.3: 14-day advance notice required using Form HR-L1.
Clause 2.4: [VERBATIM] Leave applications must receive written approval from the employee's direct manager before the leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid.
Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence will be LOP regardless of later approval.
Clause 2.6: Max 5 days carry-forward allowed; any above 5 are forfeited on 31 Dec.
Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used Jan-Mar or forfeited.
Clause 3.1: 12 paid sick leave days per calendar year.
Clause 3.2: 3+ consecutive sick days requires medical cert within 48 hours of return.
Clause 3.3: Sick leave cannot carry forward.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave adjacent to holiday/annual leave requires cert regardless of duration.
Clause 4.1: 26 weeks paid maternity for first two live births.
Clause 4.2: 12 weeks paid maternity for third+ child.
Clause 4.3: 5 days paid paternity leave within 30 days of birth.
Clause 4.4: Paternity leave cannot be split.
Clause 5.1: LWP only after exhausting all paid leave.
Clause 5.2: LWP requires approval from BOTH Department Head AND HR Director. Manager alone insufficient.
Clause 5.3: LWP >30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
Clause 5.4: LWP does not count toward service/benefits.
Clause 6.1: Entitlement to gazetted public holidays.
Clause 6.2: Work on holiday gives 1 comp off within 60 days.
Clause 6.3: Comp off cannot be encashed.
Clause 7.1: Encashment only at retirement/resignation, max 60 days.
Clause 7.2: [VERBATIM] Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
Clause 7.3: Sick leave and LWP cannot be encashed.
Clause 8.1: Grievances to HR within 10 working days of decision.
Clause 8.2: Late grievances not considered unless exceptional written circumstances.
