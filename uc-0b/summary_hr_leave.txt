CMC LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY (STRICT COMPLIANCE MODE)
==================================================

[2.3] Mandatory 14-calendar-day advance notice for annual leave using Form HR-L1.
[2.4] Written approval required before leave commences; verbal approval is strictly invalid.
[2.5] Unapproved absence is recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of late approval.
[2.6] Maximum 5 days annual leave carry-forward; excess days forfeited on 31 December.
[2.7] Carry-forward days must be used by 31 March or they are definitively forfeited.
[3.2] Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires a medical certificate within 48 hours of return.
[3.4] Medical certificate mandatory for sick leave taken immediately before or after public holidays.
[5.2] LWP requires dual approval from the Department Head AND the HR Director; manager alone is insufficient.
[5.3] LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
[7.2] Leave encashment during active service is not permitted under any circumstances.
