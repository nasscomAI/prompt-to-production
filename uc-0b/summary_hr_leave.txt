CITY MUNICIPAL CORPORATION EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY

PURPOSE AND SCOPE:
- Governs leave entitlements for permanent and contractual employees.
- Does not apply to daily wage workers or consultants.

ANNUAL LEAVE:
- 18 days paid annual leave per calendar year.
- Accrues at 1.5 days per month from joining date.
- Employees must submit leave application at least 14 calendar days in advance using Form HR-L1.
- Leave applications must receive written approval from the employee's direct manager before leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid.
- Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
- Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December.
- Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter (January–March) of the following year or they are forfeited.

SICK LEAVE:
- 12 days paid sick leave per calendar year.
- Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner, submitted within 48 hours of returning to work.
- Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the following year.
- Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday or annual leave period requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.

MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE:
- Female employees entitled to 26 weeks paid maternity leave for first two live births.
- For third or subsequent child, maternity leave is 12 weeks paid.
- Male employees entitled to 5 days paid paternity leave, to be taken within 30 days of child's birth.
- Paternity leave cannot be split across multiple periods.

LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP):
- Employee may apply for LWP only after exhausting all applicable paid leave entitlements.
- LWP requires approval from the Department Head and the HR Director. Manager approval alone is not sufficient.
- LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner.
- Periods of LWP do not count toward service for seniority, increments, or retirement benefits.

PUBLIC HOLIDAYS:
- Employees entitled to all gazetted public holidays as declared by State Government.
- If required to work on public holiday, entitled to one compensatory off day, to be taken within 60 days of holiday worked.
- Compensatory off cannot be encashed.

LEAVE ENCASHMENT:
- Annual leave may be encashed only at retirement or resignation, subject to maximum of 60 days.
- Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances.
- Sick leave and LWP cannot be encashed under any circumstances.

GRIEVANCES:
- Leave-related grievances must be raised with HR Department within 10 working days of disputed decision.
- Grievances raised after 10 working days will not be considered unless exceptional circumstances demonstrated in writing.
