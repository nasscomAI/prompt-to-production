- Clause 3.4: Sick leave before/after holiday requires certificate regardless of duration (requires)
- Clause 5.2: LWP requires approval from Department Head AND HR Director (requires)
- Clause 5.3: LWP > 30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval (requires)
