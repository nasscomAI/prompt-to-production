Clause 2.3: Employees MUST submit applications at least 14 days in advance.
Clause 2.4: [VERBATIM QUOTE - Meaning loss risk] 'Leave applications must receive written approval from the employee's direct manager before the leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid.'

Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days MUST be used Jan-Mar or they are forfeited.

Clause 3.4: Sick leave before/after a holiday REQUIRES a medical cert regardless of duration.
Clause 5.2: LWP REQUIRES approval from BOTH the Department Head AND the HR Director.
Clause 5.3: LWP >30 days REQUIRES Municipal Commissioner approval.
