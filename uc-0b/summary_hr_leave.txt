CMC EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY SUMMARY (STBA COMPLIANT)
================================================

OVERVIEW:
- Applies to permanent and contractual employees.
- Annual Leave: 18 days/year (accrues 1.5 days/month).
- Sick Leave: 12 days/year.

CRITICAL COMPLIANCE CLAUSES (MANDATORY):
- [2.3] Employees must submit leave application at least 14 calendar days in advance via Form HR-L1.
- [2.4] Written approval from direct manager is mandatory before leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid. [FLAG: VERBATIM]
- [2.5] Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval.
- [2.6] Carry forward max 5 unused annual leave days; any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 Dec.
- [2.7] Carry-forward days must be used in Q1 (Jan–Mar) or they are forfeited.
- [3.2] Sick leave of 3+ consecutive days requires a medical certificate submitted within 48 hours of return.
- [3.4] Sick leave before/after public holidays/annual leave requires a cert regardless of duration. [FLAG: VERBATIM]
- [5.2] LWP requires approval from BOTH Department Head AND HR Director. Manager approval alone is insufficient. [FLAG: VERBATIM]
- [5.3] LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner. [FLAG: VERBATIM]
- [7.2] Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances. [FLAG: VERBATIM]

OTHER ENTITLEMENTS:
- Maternity: 26 weeks (1st two births); 12 weeks thereafter.
- Paternity: 5 days (taken within 30 days of birth).
- Public Holidays: Compensatory off must be taken within 60 days if worked.
- Grievances: Must be raised within 10 working days of the decision.
