POLICY SUMMARY - CORE OBLIGATIONS

===================================

Clause 2.3: 14-day advance notice is mandatory for leave applications.
Clause 2.4: Written approval from the direct manager is required before leave commences; verbal approval is strictly invalid.
Clause 2.5: Any unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP), regardless of any subsequent approval.
Clause 2.6: A maximum of 5 unused annual leave days may be carried forward; any excess is forfeited on 31 December.
Clause 2.7: Carry-forward days must be used between January and March, or they will be forfeited.
Clause 3.2: Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate submitted within 48 hours of return.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday/annual leave requires a medical certificate regardless of duration.
Clause 5.2: Leave Without Pay (LWP) requires formal approval from BOTH the Department Head and the HR Director; manager approval alone is insufficient.
Clause 5.3: LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires additional approval from the Municipal Commissioner.
Clause 7.2: Leave encashment during active service is not permitted under any circumstances.
