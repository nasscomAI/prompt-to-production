# Policy Summary: Employee Expense Reimbursement Policy (FIN-POL-007)
Status: All specific obligations and multi-condition clauses preserved.

## Key Clauses & Obligations
- Clause 1.3: Claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense or they will NOT be processed.
- Clause 2.2: Outstation travel requires pre-approval (Form FIN-T1) (must).
- Clause 2.3: Air travel >500km allowed; Economy class is mandatory.
- Clause 2.6: DA and meal receipts cannot be claimed simultaneously for the same day (must).
- Clause 3.4: WFH equipment claims require original receipts within 60 days of written approval.
- Clause 4.4: Repayment of training (100% within 12mo, 50% between 12-24mo) if leaving CMC.
- Clause 5.3: Mobile/internet reimbursement requires original monthly bill; estimates not accepted.
- Clause 6.2: Original receipts mandatory; photocopies/screenshots accepted ONLY if physical not issued.
