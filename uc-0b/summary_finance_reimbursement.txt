HR LEAVE POLICY - BINDING OBLIGATIONS SUMMARY

Note: Clauses with complex conditions are quoted verbatim to prevent meaning loss.

- Clause 1.3: "All claims must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the expense being incurred. Claims submitted after 30 days will not be processed."
- Clause 2.2: "Outstation travel must be pre-approved using Form FIN-T1 before travel commences. Travel without prior approval is not reimbursable."
- Clause 2.6: "If actual meal expenses are claimed instead of DA, receipts are mandatory and the combined meal claim must not exceed Rs 750 per day. DA and meal receipts cannot be claimed simultaneously for the same day."
- Clause 3.4: "Claims must be submitted with original receipts within 60 days of the work-from-home arrangement being approved in writing by the Department Head."
- Clause 4.4: "If an employee leaves CMC within 12 months of a reimbursed training expense, they must repay 100% of the reimbursed amount. If they leave between 12 and 24 months, they must repay 50%."
- Clause 6.1: "All reimbursement claims must be submitted via the CMC employee portal using Form FIN-EXP1."
- Clause 6.2: "Original receipts must be attached. Photocopies and screenshots of digital receipts are accepted only if the vendor does not issue physical receipts."
- Clause 6.4: "Disputed claims must be raised with the Finance Department within 10 working days of receiving the reimbursement decision."
