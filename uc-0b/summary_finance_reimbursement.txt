# HR Leave Policy Summary

Clause 1.1: Policy governs all leave for permanent/contractual employees of CMC.
Clause 1.2: Policy does not apply to daily wage workers or consultants.
Clause 1.3 [VERBATIM - FLAG]: All claims must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the expense being incurred. Claims submitted after 30 days will not be processed.
Clause 2.1: 18 days paid annual leave per year.
Clause 2.2: Annual leave accrues 1.5 days/month from joining date.
Clause 2.3: 14-day advance notice required using Form HR-L1.
Clause 2.4: Written approval required before leave commences. Verbal not valid.
Clause 2.5: Unapproved absence = LOP regardless of subsequent approval.
Clause 2.6: Max 5 days carry-forward. Above 5 forfeited on 31 Dec.
Clause 3.1: 12 days paid sick leave per year.
Clause 3.2: 3+ consecutive sick days requires medical cert within 48hrs.
Clause 3.3: Sick leave cannot be carried forward.
Clause 3.4: Sick leave before/after holiday requires cert regardless of duration.
Clause 3.5 [VERBATIM - FLAG]: Employees on temporary or partial work-from-home arrangements are not eligible for this allowance.
Clause 4.1: 26 weeks paid maternity leave (first two births).
Clause 4.2: 12 weeks paid maternity leave (third+ birth).
Clause 4.3: 5 days paid paternity leave within 30 days of birth.
Clause 4.4: Paternity leave cannot be split.
Clause 5.1: LWP only after exhausting all applicable paid leave.
Clause 5.2: LWP requires approval from Department Head AND HR Director.
Clause 5.3: LWP >30 days requires Municipal Commissioner approval.
Clause 6.1: Entitled to gazetted public holidays declared by State Government.
Clause 6.2: Work on holiday = 1 compensatory off day within 60 days.
Clause 6.3: Compensatory off cannot be encashed.
Clause 6.4 [VERBATIM - FLAG]: Disputed claims must be raised with the Finance Department within 10 working days of receiving the reimbursement decision.
