POLICY SUMMARY: policy_finance_reimbursement
============================================================

Section 2.6: If actual meal expenses are claimed instead of DA, receipts
  are mandatory and the combined meal claim must not exceed
    Rs 750 per day. DA and meal receipts cannot be claimed
    simultaneously for the same day.

3. WORK FROM HOME EQUIPMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Section 3.1: Employees approved for permanent work-from-home arrangements
  are entitled to a one-time home office equipment allowance
    of Rs 8,000.
