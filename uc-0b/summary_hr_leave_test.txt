POLICY SUMMARY
==============

1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
--------------------
1.1 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "This policy governs all leave entitlements for permanent and contractual employees of the City Municipal Corporation (CMC)."
1.2 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "This policy does not apply to daily wage workers or consultants. Those categories are governed by their respective contracts."

2. ANNUAL LEAVE
---------------
2.1 [SUMMARIZED]: Permanent staff receive 18 days of paid annual leave annually.
2.2 [SUMMARIZED]: Annual leave accrues at 1.5 days per month from joining date.
2.3 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Employees must submit a leave application at least 14 calendar days in advance using Form HR-L1."
2.4 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Leave applications must receive written approval from the employee's direct manager before the leave commences. Verbal approval is not valid."
2.5 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Unapproved absence will be recorded as Loss of Pay (LOP) regardless of subsequent approval."
2.6 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December."
2.7 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Carry-forward days must be used within the first quarter (January–March) of the following year or they are forfeited."

3. SICK LEAVE
-------------
3.1 [SUMMARIZED]: Staff receive 12 days of paid sick leave annually.
3.2 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Sick leave of 3 or more consecutive days requires a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner, submitted within 48 hours of returning to work."
3.3 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the following year."
3.4 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Sick leave taken immediately before or after a public holiday or annual leave period requires a medical certificate regardless of duration."

4. MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE
--------------------------------
4.1 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave for the first two live births."
4.2 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "For a third or subsequent child, maternity leave is 12 weeks paid."
4.3 [SUMMARIZED]: Male staff receive 5 days of paid paternity leave, to be taken within 30 days of the child's birth.
4.4 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Paternity leave cannot be split across multiple periods."

5. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP)
--------------------------
5.1 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "An employee may apply for Leave Without Pay only after exhausting all applicable paid leave entitlements."
5.2 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "LWP requires approval from the Department Head and the HR Director. Manager approval alone is not sufficient."
5.3 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "LWP exceeding 30 continuous days requires approval from the Municipal Commissioner."
5.4 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Periods of LWP do not count toward service for the purposes of seniority, increments, or retirement benefits."

6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
------------------
6.1 [SUMMARIZED]: Employees are entitled to all gazetted public holidays as declared by the State Government each year.
6.2 [SUMMARIZED]: If an employee is required to work on a public holiday, they are entitled to one compensatory off day, to be taken within 60 days of the holiday worked.
6.3 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Compensatory off cannot be encashed."

7. LEAVE ENCASHMENT
-------------------
7.1 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Annual leave may be encashed only at the time of retirement or resignation, subject to a maximum of 60 days."
7.2 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Leave encashment during service is not permitted under any circumstances."
7.3 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Sick leave and LWP cannot be encashed under any circumstances."

8. GRIEVANCES
-------------
8.1 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Leave-related grievances must be raised with the HR Department within 10 working days of the disputed decision."
8.2 [VERBATIM/COMPLEX]: "Grievances raised after 10 working days will not be considered unless exceptional circumstances are demonstrated in writing."
