Q: Can I carry forward unused annual leave?
A: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave     days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are     forfeited on 31 December. [Source: policy_hr_leave.txt Section 2.6]
----------------------------------------
Q: Can I install Slack on my work laptop?
A: Employees must not install software on corporate devices     without written approval from the IT Department. [Source: policy_it_acceptable_use.txt Section 2.3]
----------------------------------------
Q: What is the home office equipment allowance?
A: Employees approved for permanent work-from-home arrangements     are entitled to a one-time home office equipment allowance     of Rs 8,000. [Source: policy_finance_reimbursement.txt Section 3.1]
----------------------------------------
Q: Can I use my personal phone for work files from home?
A: Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the     CMC employee self-service portal only. [Source: policy_it_acceptable_use.txt Section 3.1]
