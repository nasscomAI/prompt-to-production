HR Leave Policy — Hyderabad Civic Tech Org

1. Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per calendar year. Leave must be applied for at least 7 days in advance. Unused leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.

2. Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. Medical certificate must be provided for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days. Sick leave cannot be encashed.

3. Maternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave. This benefit is available only after completion of 80 days of service in the 12 months preceding the expected delivery date.

4. Emergency Leave
Employees may take up to 3 days of emergency leave per year for immediate family emergencies. Approval must be obtained from the reporting manager within 24 hours of taking leave.
