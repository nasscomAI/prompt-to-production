This document contains the verified responses to the 7 mandatory test questions

Q1: "Can I carry forward unused annual leave?"
A: Per policy_hr_leave.txt section 2.6: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days. Section 2.7: These must be used within the first quarter (January-March) or they are forfeited.

Q2: "Can I install Slack on my work laptop?"
A: Per policy_it_acceptable_use.txt section 2.3: Employees must not install software on corporate devices without written approval from the IT Department.

Q3: "What is the home office equipment allowance?"
A: Per policy_finance_reimbursement.txt section 3.1: Employees approved for permanent work-from-home are entitled to a one-time allowance of Rs 8,000. Section 3.5: Temporary or partial WFH arrangements are not eligible.

Q4: "Can I use my personal phone for work files from home?"
A: Per policy_it_acceptable_use.txt section 3.1: Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the CMC employee self-service portal only. They must not be used to access, store, or transmit other CMC data.

Q5: "What is the company view on flexible working culture?"
A: This question is not covered in the available policy documents
(policy_hr_leave.txt, policy_it_acceptable_use.txt, policy_finance_reimbursement.txt).
Please contact [relevant team] for guidance.

Q6: "Can I claim DA and meal receipts on the same day?"
A: Per policy_finance_reimbursement.txt section 2.6: DA and meal receipts cannot be claimed simultaneously for the same day. Receipts are mandatory if actual meals are claimed instead of DA.

Q7: "Who approves leave without pay?"
A: Per policy_hr_leave.txt section 5.2: LWP requires approval from both the Department Head AND the HR Director. Manager approval alone is not sufficient.
