Q: Can I carry forward unused annual leave?
A: [policy_hr_leave.txt Section 2.6]: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December.

Q: Can I install Slack on my work laptop?
A: [policy_it_acceptable_use.txt Section 2.3]: Employees must not install non-standard or third-party software on CMC laptops without prior written approval from the IT Helpdesk.

Q: What is the home office equipment allowance?
A: [policy_finance_reimbursement.txt Section 3.1]: Employees designated as permanent WFH are eligible for a one-time home office equipment allowance up to Rs 8,000.
