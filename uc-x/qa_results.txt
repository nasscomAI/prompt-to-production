Q: Can I carry forward unused annual leave?
A: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December. (policy_hr_leave.txt, Section 2.6)

Q: Can I install Slack on my work laptop?
A: Employees must not install software on corporate devices without written approval from the IT Department. (policy_it_acceptable_use.txt, Section 2.3)

Q: What is the home office equipment allowance?
A: Employees approved for permanent work-from-home arrangements are entitled to a one-time home office equipment allowance of Rs 8,000. (policy_finance_reimbursement.txt, Section 3.1)
----------------------------------------
Q: Can I use my personal phone for work files from home?
A: Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the CMC employee self-service portal only. (policy_it_acceptable_use.txt, Section 3.1)
