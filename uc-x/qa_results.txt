Q: Can I carry forward unused annual leave?
A:
2.6 Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave
    days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are
    forfeited on 31 December.
[Source: policy_hr_leave.txt, Section 2.6]

Q: Can I claim DA and meal receipts on the same day?
A:
2.6 If actual meal expenses are claimed instead of DA, receipts
    are mandatory and the combined meal claim must not exceed
    Rs 750 per day. DA and meal receipts cannot be claimed
    simultaneously for the same day.
