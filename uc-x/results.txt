1. Q: Can I carry forward unused annual leave?
   A: Employees may carry forward a maximum of 5 unused annual leave days to the following calendar year. Any days above 5 are forfeited on 31 December. [Document: policy_hr_leave.txt, Section: 2.6]

2. Q: Can I install Slack on my work laptop?
   A: Employees must not install software on corporate devices without written approval from the IT Department. [Document: policy_it_acceptable_use.txt, Section: 2.3]

3. Q: What is the home office equipment allowance?
   A: Employees approved for permanent work-from-home arrangements are entitled to a one-time home office equipment allowance of Rs 8,000. [Document: policy_finance_reimbursement.txt, Section: 3.1]

4. Q: Can I use my personal phone to access work files when working from home?
   A: Personal devices may be used to access CMC email and the CMC employee self-service portal only. [Document: policy_it_acceptable_use.txt, Section: 3.1]
